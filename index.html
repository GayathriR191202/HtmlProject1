Gayu's Favourite Rose

Roses have a long and colourful history.
They have been symbols of love,beauty,war and politics.
The rose is,according to fossil evidence,35 million years old.
In nature,the Genus Rosa has some 150 species spread throughout the Nothern Hemisphere,from Alaska to Mexico and including northern Africa.

If you want to learn more about the Rose,check out its wikipedia page.

Roses are famous because...

  1. Roses are best known as ornamental plants grown for their flowers in the garden and sometimes indoors.
  2. They have been also used for commercial perfumery and commercial cut flower crops.
  3. Some are used as landscape plants,for hedging and for other utilitarian purposes such as game cover and slope stabilization.
